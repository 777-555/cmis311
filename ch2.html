Chapter 2 ”Developing strong listening and communication skills“ focuses on how to communicate well with customers. It explains how to convey sensitive information. It describes how to speak properly and use the appropriate tone. It also explains how good posture and breathing helps influence intonation. This chapter provides tips on how to communicate with each type of customer in a positive way. The main theme is that good listening and speaking skills help provide better customer service.
GLOSSARY Review Questions paraphrase—To restate the information given by a customer using slightly different words in an effort to verify that you understand. social listening—The process of identifying and evaluating what is being said about a company, individual, product, or brand on the Internet; also known as social media listening.
