This chapter explains how to handle difficult customers in a professional way. Most customers are friendly, but some may feel dissatisfied or angry. Staying positive and calm is very helpful in these situations. Professionals should focus on solutions and avoid reacting emotionally. Techniques like deep breathing can help reduce stress. With practice, handling difficult customers becomes easier, and even unhappy customers can become loyal ones.
